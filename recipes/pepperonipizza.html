Pepperoni Pizza

Pizza is one of America's favortie foods. And you know what makes it even better? Pepperroni man. This recipe takes just 30 minutes and the end result is a delicious baked and seasoned pizza. Perfect for nights when you have very little time and need to feed the family and get them out the door, this pepperoni pizza wil leave everyone full and satisfied.

Ingredients

  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Dough
  • Red sauce
  • Pepperoni slices
  • Basil
  • Salt and Pepper
  1. Take the dough, knead it, and begin shaping it into a round form
  2. Preheat your oven to 370 degrees
  3. Spread the marinara sauce evenly over the dough, while sprinkling in some salt and pepper
  4. Time to place the star ingredient, pepperoni, by spreading it over the sauce
  5. Next sprinkle a heavy amount of both cheeses over the sauce. It is importnat to spread cheese evenly so that no sections of the pizza contain burnt cheese
  6. Sprinkle some fresh oregano over the pizza
  7. Put in the oven for 30 minutes
  8. At 25 minutes, check to make sure crust edges aren't burnt. Cook for the remaining 5 minutes as you see fit
