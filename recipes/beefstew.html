Beef Stew

Nothing beats the cold better than a warm and hearty pot of stew. As we fall deeper in winter, having access to these warm and nutritiuos foods is wonderful! This recipe will instruct you what ingredients and how to make beef stew. It's a healthy alternative that doesn't require a lot of prepping. Whip this up ina crockpot during the cold and you'll be eating good all winter

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs of Beef
  • Beef Broth
  • Carrots
  • Potatoes
  • Onions
  • Crockpot
  • Olive oil
  1. After thawing out the beef, place in a high heat pan filled with olive oil and sear all the sides of the meat- 5 minutes per side
  2. Remove the beef from the heat and place in your crockpot
  3. On the side, dice your carrots and potatoes
  4. Cut the onions into larger peels/slices. They will play a big role in acting as a marinade for the stew mixture
  5. Pour the carrots, potatoes, and onions into the crockpot, which already contains the meat
  6. Pour in as much beef broth as you see fit into the pot
  7. Set the crockpot heat to low and set a timer for 10 hours, while keeping the pot lid closed
  8. 8 hours into the simmering, open the lid and pull apart or flay the beef
  9. Shut crockpot heat off after timer is done and make sure that the vegetables are tender and soft enough
